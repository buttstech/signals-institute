Pillar 1: Youth Exposure & Career Development

We serve youth ages 10-18 through two tracks. For younger students (10-14), we bring working researchers directly into schools and community spaces—every session anchored by a hands-on activity that surfaces a real concept through doing. For high school students (14-18), we offer structured career development: project-based programs, internship placements, and a pathway to the Signals Youth Research Journal.

Pillar 2: Veteran Transition

Veterans bring clearances, technical foundations, and mission orientation. We build individualized transition pathways—not fixed programs—drawing from content areas in professional transition, RF and defense technology, and AI and software tools. Every pathway is custom to the veteran's background and goals.

Pillar 3: Community Engagement

Open to all—no prerequisites, no application. Monthly Researcher Forums, community workshops, and technical tool sessions build dialogue, transparency, and accessible technical skill across the broader Riviera Beach community.
