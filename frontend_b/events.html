Riviera27 is the Applied Research Summit — a public convening of educators, scientists, defense professionals, and civic leaders. Targeted for December 2027 in Riviera Beach, Florida.

Four Session Tracks:

• Applied Systems & Defense
• Scientific Implementation & Policy
• Education & Community Interface
• Ethics & Responsibility

Participation:

• Attend as researcher, educator, or practitioner
• Sponsor the summit or specific tracks
• Join the advisory council
• Submit for speaking consideration
